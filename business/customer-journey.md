```markdown
# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: User searches for "free Docker tutorials" or "Docker project templates" online.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of search results, difficulty in finding comprehensive and free resources.
- **User Emotions**: Frustrated, overwhelmed, hopeful.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide a curated list of top Docker resources directly on the search results page.
- **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to the Docker-Craft landing page.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: User visits Docker-Craft website and explores the available resources.
- **Friction Points**: Unclear value proposition, lack of testimonials or social proof.
- **User Emotions**: Curious, skeptical, interested.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Showcase success stories or testimonials from other Docker learners.
- **Metric**: Time spent on the Docker-Craft website.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: User signs up for a free account to access Docker-Craft resources.
- **Friction Points**: Complex sign-up process, lack of immediate access to resources.
- **User Emotions**: Excited, impatient, hopeful.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer a quick start guide or a sample project immediately upon sign-up.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from visitor to registered user.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: User starts using Docker-Craft resources regularly for learning and practice.
- **Friction Points**: Difficulty in navigating the platform, lack of personalized recommendations.
- **User Emotions**: Engaged, satisfied, motivated.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide personalized learning paths based on user's progress and interests.
- **Metric**: Frequency of resource usage and completion rates of tutorials.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: User becomes proficient in Docker and starts looking for advanced resources or community engagement.
- **Friction Points**: Limited advanced content, lack of community interaction.
- **User Emotions**: Accomplished, eager to learn more, seeking community.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer advanced courses, webinars, or a community forum for Docker enthusiasts.
- **Metric**: Number of advanced courses completed and community engagement metrics.
```
