# Business Model Canvas
## Customer Segments
* **Primary Customer Segments**:
	+ Docker learners (individuals and teams)
	+ Developers and engineers interested in containerization
	+ Students and educators in computer science and related fields
* **Secondary Customer Segments**:
	+ IT professionals and DevOps teams
	+ Businesses and organizations using Docker in their infrastructure
* **Target Market Size**: TAM (Total Addressable Market) = $1,200,000,000 USD, SAM (Serviceable Available Market) = $450,000,000 USD, SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market) = $150,000,000 USD

## Value Propositions
* **Unique Selling Points**:
	+ Comprehensive and free Docker project resources
	+ Tutorials, templates, and practice environments for learners
	+ Easy access to Docker expertise and community support
* **Key Benefits**:
	+ Improved learning outcomes and skills development
	+ Increased productivity and efficiency in Docker projects
	+ Access to a community of Docker enthusiasts and experts
* **Pain Points Addressed**:
	+ Limited free resources for practicing Docker projects
	+ Difficulty in finding comprehensive and reliable Docker tutorials and templates

## Channels
* **Primary Channels**:
	+ Online platform (docker-craft.io)
	+ Social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub)
	+ Community forums and discussion groups
* **Secondary Channels**:
	+ Partnerships with Docker-related businesses and organizations
	+ Collaborations with educators and educational institutions
	+ Influencer marketing and outreach
* **Target Audience Reach**: 10,000+ Docker learners and enthusiasts per month

## Customer Relationships
* **Primary Relationships**:
	+ Community engagement and support
	+ Personalized learning experiences and feedback
	+ Regular updates and new content releases
* **Secondary Relationships**:
	+ Partnerships and collaborations with Docker-related businesses
	+ Educational and research partnerships
	+ Influencer and ambassador programs
* **Relationship Building**: 80% of customers engage with the community and support team

## Revenue Streams
* **Primary Revenue Streams**:
	+ Advertising and sponsored content
	+ Affiliate marketing and partnerships
	+ Premium features and services (e.g., expert mentorship)
* **Secondary Revenue Streams**:
	+ Data analytics and insights
	+ Sponsored content and product placements
	+ Event and conference sponsorships
* **Revenue Growth**: 20% month-over-month growth in revenue

## Key Resources
* **Primary Resources**:
	+ Docker expertise and community support
	+ Comprehensive and free Docker project resources
	+ Online platform and infrastructure
* **Secondary Resources**:
	+ Partnerships with Docker-related businesses and organizations
	+ Educational and research partnerships
	+ Influencer and ambassador programs
* **Resource Allocation**: 60% of resources dedicated to community support and engagement

## Key Activities
* **Primary Activities**:
	+ Community engagement and support
	+ Content creation and curation
	+ Platform maintenance and updates
* **Secondary Activities**:
	+ Partnerships and collaborations with Docker-related businesses
	+ Educational and research partnerships
	+ Influencer and ambassador programs
* **Activity Frequency**: 4 times a week

## Key Partners
* **Primary Partners**:
	+ Docker Inc.
	+ Docker-related businesses and organizations
	+ Educational and research institutions
* **Secondary Partners**:
	+ Influencers and ambassadors
	+ Affiliate marketing partners
	+ Sponsored content partners
* **Partner Engagement**: 80% of partners engage with the community and support team

## Cost Structure
* **Fixed Costs**:
	+ Platform maintenance and updates ($10,000/month)
	+ Community support and engagement ($5,000/month)
	+ Marketing and advertising ($3,000/month)
* **Variable Costs**:
	+ Content creation and curation ($2,000/month)
	+ Partnerships and collaborations ($1,500/month)
	+ Influencer and ambassador programs ($1,000/month)
* **Cost Savings**: 20% reduction in costs through efficient resource allocation
